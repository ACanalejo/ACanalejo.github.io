My name is Álvaro Canalejo-Molero and I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the [University of Lucerne](https://www.unilu.ch/en/), as part of the research project [DIGIPOL](https://www.unilu.ch/en/faculties/faculty-of-humanities-and-social-sciences/institutes-departements-and-research-centres/department-of-political-science/research/digitalization-and-political-conflict-parties-voters-and-electoral-alignment-digipol/). I am also a PhD researcher at the [European University Institute](https://www.eui.eu/en/home), where I am finishing my dissertation.

Substantively, I am interested in comparative party politics, public opinion and behavior, but I am also very keen to the most recent advances in quantitative methodology.

Thanks for the visit!
