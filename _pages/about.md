My name is Álvaro Canalejo-Molero and I am a PhD candidate at the [European University Institute](https://www.eui.eu/en/home). Previously, I studied my Bachelor at the University Pablo de Olavide of Seville and my master at the [IC3JM](https://ic3jm.es/en/) of the University Carlos III of Madrid. I have also been a visiting researcher at the [European Institute](https://www.lse.ac.uk/european-institute) of the [London School of Economics](https://www.lse.ac.uk/).

Under the supervision of Prof. Hanspeter Kriesi, my thesis looks at how different indicators of democratic engagement affects and get affected by new party entry in Western democracies. Besides comparative politics, parties and behavior, I'm interested in causal inference designs and computational methods applied to social science.

Thanks for the visit!
