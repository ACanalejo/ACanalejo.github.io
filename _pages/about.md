I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the [University of Lucerne](https://www.unilu.ch/en/), where I work in the SNF-funded project [DIGIPOL](https://www.unilu.ch/en/faculties/faculty-of-humanities-and-social-sciences/institutes-departements-and-research-centres/department-of-political-science/research/digitalization-and-political-conflict-parties-voters-and-electoral-alignment-digipol/). I am also a member of the [Digital Society Initiative of the Univeristy of Zurich](https://www.dsi.uzh.ch/en.html) and I hold a [PhD](https://hdl.handle.net/1814/75692) from the [European University Institute](https://www.eui.eu/en/home).

I am broadly interested in comparative politics, political behavior and public opinion. Moreover, I am genuinely interested in the most recent advances in social research methodology.

Thanks for the visit!
