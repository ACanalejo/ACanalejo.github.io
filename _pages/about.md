My name is Álvaro Canalejo-Molero. I am PhD candidate at the [European University Institute](https://www.eui.eu/en/home). Previously, I studied at the University Pablo de Olavide of Sevill and made my master at the [IC3JM](https://ic3jm.es/en/) of the University Carlos III of Madrid.

Under the supervision of Prof. Hanspeter Kriesi, my thesis looks at how different indicators of democratic engagement affects and get affected by new party entry in Western democracies. Apart from comparative party politics and political behavior, I am also interested in causal inference and computational methods applied to social science.

Thanks for the visit!
