My name is Álvaro Canalejo-Molero and I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the [University of Lucerne](https://www.unilu.ch/en/), as part of the research team [DIGIPOL](https://www.unilu.ch/en/faculties/faculty-of-humanities-and-social-sciences/institutes-departements-and-research-centres/department-of-political-science/research/digitalization-and-political-conflict-parties-voters-and-electoral-alignment-digipol/). Previously, I defended [my PhD dissertation](https://hdl.handle.net/1814/75692) at the [European University Institute](https://www.eui.eu/en/home).

I am broadly interested in comparative politics, political behavior and public opinion. Moreover, I am genuinely interested in the most recent advances in quantitative social science methodology.

Thanks for the visit!
