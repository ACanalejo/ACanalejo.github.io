My name is Álvaro Canalejo-Molero. I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the [University of Lucerne](https://www.unilu.ch/en/). As part of the research project [DIGIPOL](https://www.unilu.ch/en/faculties/faculty-of-humanities-and-social-sciences/institutes-departements-and-research-centres/department-of-political-science/research/digitalization-and-political-conflict-parties-voters-and-electoral-alignment-digipol/), I study how Swiss political parties structure nascent conflicts over digitalization issues.  

I am also a PhD candidate at the [European University Institute](https://www.eui.eu/en/home), where I work on the consequences of new party entry and party system change for political engagement. 

Besides comparative politics, political sociology and behavior, I'm interested in quantitative social science methodology, especially in causal inference designs and computational methods.

Thanks for the visit!
