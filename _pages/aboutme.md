I am a political scientist from Córdoba (Spain). Since 2018 I am a PhD researcher/candidate at the European University Institute of Florence (Italy) under the supervision of Prof. Hanspeter Kriesi.

My research looks at the intersection of party politics, public opinion and political behavior. It explores the underlying attitudinal factors explaining new party entry in established democracies and the attitudinal and behavioral change provoked by party system change.

My work has a strongly quantitative approach, with particular focus in causal inference designs. Lately, I have become increasingly interested in computational methods applied to social sciences.

I also have a broad interest in political socialization and the the social consequences of public publicy.

Please, do not hesitate to [contact me](mailto:alvaro.canalejo@eui.eu) for any inquiry or research proposal!
