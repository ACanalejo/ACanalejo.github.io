I am a political scientist from Córdoba (Spain). Since December 2022 I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the project DIGIPOL, where I study the politicization of digitalization issues in Switzerland. I am also a PhD candidate at the European University Institute of Florence (Italy), where I study the consequences of new party entry for political engagement.

My research looks at the intersection of party politics, public opinion and behavior, and it can be broadly conceived as political sociology. I am interested in two specific topics. First, I study how changes in the party system affect political attitudes and behavior, with special emphasis on expressions of democratic engagement. Second, I am increasingly interested on how political parties incorporate issues with cross-cutting conflict potential into the political agenda, particularly digitalization issues.

My work has a strong quantitative approach, and incorporates experimental and causal inference designs with computational methods.
