I am a political scientist from Córdoba (Spain). Since December 2022 I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Lucerne, in the research project DIGIPOL. As part of this project, I study the politicization of digitalization issues in Switzerland. 

Since June 2023, I am a Doctor in Political and Social Sciences by the European University Institute of Florence (Italy). In my PhD dissertation, I studied the consequences of new party entry for political engagement under the supervision of Prof. Hanspeter Kriesi.

My research looks at the intersection of party politics, public opinion and behavior, and it can be broadly conceived as political sociology. My work has a strong quantitative approach, and incorporates experimental and causal inference designs with computational methods.
