This is a course for undergraduate and graduate students that I taught at the Univeristy of Lucerne during the Spring term of 2022. The course is an intensive two-wekeends seminar that reviews the different 'transformation waves' that have shaped Western European party systems from the 1960s until now. It also provides an overview of the main demand and supply-side factors discussed in the literature to make sense of these changes. The goal is to provide students with conceptual and empirical tools to analyse the evolution of Western European party systems from a historical and comparative perspective. The repository of the course can be found [here](https://github.com/ACanalejo/cwps_continuity_and_change_unilu2022).
